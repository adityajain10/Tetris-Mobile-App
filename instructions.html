    INSTRUCTIONS


    Click the START button to play. Press P to pause or re-start the game. Use the arrow keys to move the pieces. The up-arrow or Z rotates the piece. The left and right arrows move the piece to the left or right, respectively. The down arrow moves the piece down faster, and X moves the piece all the way down immediately. Horizontal lines that are filled completely are removed from the stack, and the score is incremented.
